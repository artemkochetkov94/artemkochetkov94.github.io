OMG!
about us

At OMG, we provide specialized recruiting services within the Merchant Services Industry.

We understand that every company is unique, and we spend time getting to know your organization and its culture.

Our job doesn’t stop with the hire! We help to ensure the success of each of our placements by maintaining an active role during their guarantee period and training process.

In addition to assigning you a specialized Account Manager, we provide taylor made recruitment programs and custom payment models to each of our clients.

Our Story
story

The story of Opportune is one of coworkers uniting under the realization that they have the power to improve how their industry functions, and to empower business owners.

Our recruiting team has had the usual ups and downs that are standard within the industry, but early on we noticed a few problems. Our ISOs were having trouble affording recruiting services and had no idea where to find good sales people and support staff on their own. Then, they didn't have the time to fully train their new recruits.

One day, we were sitting around discussing these issues, and...

OMG!!!

We realized that we could provide a simple yet robust, cost-effective solution! With combined experience including sales, staffing agency management, start-up consultation, and various roles within merchant services, we perceived that creating a specialized industry firm with custom recruiting, training, and pricing options would solve this problem plaguing our industry!

We are excited to be launching this revolutionary recruiting group, and we look forward to helping our clients scale up!

The OMG Way
way
